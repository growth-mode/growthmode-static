FRACTIONAL AI CONSULTANCY

Senior AI expertise, embedded in your business. Without the full-time hire.

Baz Furby, founder of Growth Mode, works directly with a small number of businesses as a fractional AI consultant. This is not a course. It is not advisory from a distance. It is embedded, hands-on work — identifying where AI creates the most leverage in your business, building the processes, and training your team to run them.

Discovery & AI Audit

A structured review of your current operations, tools, and workflows to identify where AI can drive the most impact.

Process Design & Build

Designing and building AI-powered processes tailored to your business — from automation workflows to decision-support systems.

Team Training & Enablement

Practical, hands-on training for your team so they can run, maintain, and extend the AI systems we build together.

Ongoing Strategic Guidance

Continued strategic support as your AI capabilities mature — identifying new opportunities, refining processes, and keeping you ahead.

WHO THIS IS FOR

  • CEOs and founders at growth-stage businesses who want to move fast on AI but need experienced guidance.
  • Leadership teams building an AI strategy without an internal AI function.
  • Businesses that have tried AI tools but haven't seen the commercial impact they expected.
