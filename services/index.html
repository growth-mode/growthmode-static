HOW GROWTH MODE WORKS

Every engagement starts with discovery. We audit your current stack, map your workflows, and score each part of your business against AI leverage potential. The output is a prioritised plan — not a list of tools to evaluate, but a concrete sequence of interventions with expected outcomes.

From there, we move into build. Whether that is a full AI product, an automation system, a content engine, or an embedded consultancy arrangement, the work is hands-on and founder-led. Baz is involved throughout — not managing delivery from a distance, but doing the work alongside your team.

We work with B2B companies across martech, adtech, and fintech, primarily in the UK but also internationally. Engagements are intentionally limited in number so each one gets the depth of attention it needs. WHY AI-FIRST MATTERS IN 2026

  • Businesses built around AI-native products and processes have a structural cost advantage over those retrofitting AI onto legacy operations.
  • Search has changed. AI Overviews, answer engines, and zero-click results mean the old SEO playbook is no longer enough — and most B2B companies haven't adapted yet.
  • The automation gap between early adopters and laggards is widening. Teams using AI to handle repetitive work are compounding that advantage every month.
  • The window for building AI as a competitive moat is open now. In three years, it will be table stakes. The businesses that move first are the ones that get to define the standard.
