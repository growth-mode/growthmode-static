AI AUTOMATION AND TRAINING

Automate the work. Train the team. Scale without headcount.

Most businesses are using AI as a search engine. The ones pulling ahead are using it as an operating system. There's a material difference between a team that uses ChatGPT to write emails and a business where AI is embedded into the core workflows — running lead qualification, generating reports, producing content, and surfacing decisions before they're needed. We close that gap. We audit your operations, build the automation, and train your team to run it.

AI Audit

A structured review of your current operations, tools, workflows, and data sources to identify where AI creates the most commercial leverage. Output: a prioritised AI implementation roadmap with estimated impact per workstream — not a theoretical framework, a build list.

AI Training Workshops

Practical, hands-on workshops for teams at every level. Not theory sessions about what AI might do — live sessions where your team builds and deploys real workflows during the workshop itself. We cover prompt engineering, AI tool selection, workflow design, and output quality control.

AI Chief of Staff

An AI-powered chief of staff built and embedded directly into your business operations. Monitors priorities, prepares meeting briefs, surfaces anomalies in data, tracks action items, and generates executive summaries. Built on Claude agents with integrations into your calendar, CRM, and reporting stack.

Content Production Systems

End-to-end AI content workflows — from ideation and research through to publication. Research agents that monitor competitors and surface topics. Draft generation that matches your brand voice. Editing workflows that maintain quality at scale. Deployed for B2B SaaS, fintech, and martech clients.

AI Dashboards & Analysis Workflows

Custom AI-powered dashboards that pull from your data sources — CRM, analytics, finance, product usage — surface the metrics that matter, and generate automated insights and anomaly alerts. No more building reports manually. No more waiting for the analyst to finish before making a decision.

WHERE AI ADDS MOST VALUE

The businesses getting the biggest returns from AI are not the ones using the most tools. They're the ones who've identified the two or three workflows where AI can 10x output or remove a human bottleneck entirely — and then built those properly.

The most common high-impact areas we find in audits: outbound sales sequences and lead qualification, content production and publishing, data analysis and reporting, customer support triage, and internal knowledge management. The specific wins vary by business — the audit tells us where yours are.

1. AI Audit

We map your current operations and identify the highest-value automation opportunities. Delivered as a prioritised implementation roadmap within two weeks.

2. Workflow Design

We design the automation architecture — tools, triggers, agents, integrations, and quality controls — before building anything. You see and approve the blueprint first.

3. Build & Deploy

We build the automations, connect them to your existing stack, test edge cases, and deploy. You get working systems, not prototypes.

4. Train & Hand Over

Your team learns to run, manage, and extend the systems we've built. Documentation, training sessions, and ongoing support included.
