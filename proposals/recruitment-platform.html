BESPOKE AI-FIRST RECRUITMENT PLATFORM

A Growth Mode Proposal for Pagos Consultants

01THE POSITION

We don't build Frankenstein stacks. We architect unified, AI-native recruitment operating systems — where every module is purpose-built, and every module talks.

This is not a configuration of existing SaaS tools. This is a bespoke platform built around how your business operates — not how a vendor decided you should.

The next generation of recruitment businesses will be built on unified, AI-native platforms that own the full workflow from sourcing through to placement. Fewer tools. Lower operational drag. Faster placements. Higher recruiter output.

The goal is simple: fewer tools, lower drag, faster placements, higher output.

The Brief

What follows is our response. Not a pitch deck. Not a product demo. A technical proposal for building the recruitment operating system that doesn't exist yet — because it needs to be built around you.

02WHAT YOU ASKED FOR — AND WHAT WE'D BUILD

Core modules, each built as a clean API-connected component. No bolt-ons. No Zapier glue. Every module shares a single data layer and communicates through a central AI orchestration engine.

Unified CRM/ATS
A single data model — no bolt-ons. Candidates, clients, jobs, and placements in one schema. Not two systems pretending to be one.
Native Sourcing
Internal database with AI-powered re-surfacing + external reach via LinkedIn, CV databases, GitHub and more. Your database becomes a living asset, not a graveyard.
Multi-Channel Sequencing
LinkedIn, email, SMS — with AI-personalised messaging at each touchpoint. Sequences that adapt based on engagement signals, not static templates.
Data Enrichment
Email, phone, company intel — auto-enriched on every new record via API. Apollo, Hunter, Clearbit and custom sources unified into a single enrichment layer.
Market Mapping
Visualise talent pools, competitor organisations, hiring trends — not just pipeline boards. Strategic intelligence built into the recruiter workflow.
VOIP + Calling
Integrated calling with AI call transcription, summary, and automatic CRM logging. Every call generates structured data, not just a note.
Workflow Automation
Trigger-based AND agentic — the AI doesn't just move cards, it takes next-best-actions. Automation that thinks, not just reacts.
Performance Intelligence
Recruiter output dashboards, KPI tracking, activity attribution — built for operators, not vanity. Know which behaviours drive placements.
Gamification Engine
Leaderboards, streaks, targets, team challenges — built into the daily UX. Competitive energy channelled into measurable output.
Clean UX
A recruiter-first interface that people actually want to use daily. Speed, clarity, and zero friction — designed around how recruiters actually work.

03THE GROWTH MODE LAYER

Beyond what you asked for. This is what makes the platform genuinely next-generation — and what no off-the-shelf tool will ever deliver.

AI-First Means Two Things

AGENTIC
Takes action on behalf of its users
CONVERSATIONAL
Ask it anything about your business

AI Copilot

CONVERSATIONAL AI ACROSS EVERY MODULE

Not a chatbot bolted on. A conversational layer running through the entire platform. “Who matches this JD and hasn't been contacted in 90 days?” “What's our average time-to-placement for senior finance roles in Q1?” “Draft an outreach sequence for this candidate.” The AI knows your full business context.

Agentic Automation

AI THAT ACTS, NOT JUST REACTS

The AI doesn't wait to be told. Spots a candidate who opened an email 3 times — schedules a call task + drafts a LinkedIn message. Detects a stalled job — flags it, suggests re-sourcing actions. Identifies growing headcount at a target account — surfaces it as a warm BD opportunity.

Intent Signal Monitoring

PASSIVE PIPELINE INTELLIGENCE

Watch your entire talent pool and client base for real-time signals. CV updates, LinkedIn profile changes, companies posting multiple roles, contacts changing jobs. Pipeline intelligence that never sleeps.

AI Job Spec Intelligence

FROM SPEC TO SHORTLIST IN SECONDS

Feed in a job spec. AI auto-extracts requirements, scores your existing database, generates a candidate shortlist, and drafts the candidate-facing brief. No manual interpretation required.

Candidate Re-engagement

MINE THE GOLDMINE YOU ALREADY HAVE

Most ATS databases are goldmines nobody mines. An AI layer that continuously re-surfaces dormant candidates against live roles — with personalised reactivation sequences triggered automatically.

Competitive Intelligence

KNOW YOUR MARKET IN REAL-TIME

Who are your clients hiring from? Which competitors are placing into your target accounts? A market intelligence feed built directly into the platform — keeping your team commercially aware at all times.

Revenue Attribution

CONNECT ACTIONS TO PLACEMENTS

Not just activity dashboards. Connect every recruiter action — call, email, LinkedIn touch — back to a placement. Know exactly which behaviours drive revenue and build training around real data.

Client Portal

PREMIUM CLIENT EXPERIENCE

A branded, real-time portal where clients view shortlists, provide feedback, approve candidates, and track process. Reduces email back-and-forth and positions your business as premium.

Unified Inbox

EVERY CONVERSATION, ONE FEED

All recruiter communications — LinkedIn, email, SMS, VOIP notes — in one feed per candidate and per client. No context-switching. No lost conversations. Complete relationship history.

Onboarding & Compliance

CLOSE THE LOOP AUTOMATICALLY

Once a placement is made — automate reference checking, right-to-work verification, offer management, and contractor/perm onboarding flows. The full lifecycle, not just sourcing to offer.
